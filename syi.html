Soweto Youth Initiative

For a month I volunteered at Soweto Youth Initiative in the slums of Nairobi, Kenia. I helped them set up a website to promote their work and took photos to support this goal. It was truly a once in a lifetime experience and I am super impressed by the work they do. If you have a spare minute, visit their website at www.sowetoyouthinitiative.org
